# Team & Partners

MetaHoof is developed and operated by **ANP Labs**, a company focused on building and maintaining structured digital systems for competitive gameplay.

ANP Labs is responsible for the long-term operation of the MetaHoof ecosystem, including:

- system design and evolution
- economic balance and sustainability
- infrastructure and platform development

The team combines experience across game design, blockchain infrastructure, and product development, with a focus on building systems that remain functional and balanced over time.

## ANP Labs

![hero](/img/anp_labs.png)

**ANP Labs** is a registered technology company based in Brazil and the operator of the MetaHoof system.

Its role is not only to develop the game, but to maintain the integrity of the underlying economy and ensure that participation, competition, and constraints remain aligned.

Key responsibilities include:

- **System Development & Operation**  
  Designing and maintaining the game systems, including racing mechanics, agent execution, and economic structures.

- **Economic Oversight**  
  Monitoring and adjusting parameters to preserve balance between participation, rewards, and constraints.

- **Infrastructure & Security**  
  Ensuring reliable operation, asset integrity, and secure interaction with the platform.

- **Ecosystem Growth**  
  Expanding participation and integrating new environments while maintaining a unified system.
